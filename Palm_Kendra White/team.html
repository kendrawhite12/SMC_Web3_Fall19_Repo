Our Team

Palm founders Dorothy Day and Brighdie Grounds (centerd) are making waves in the fashion space and on the philanthropic front. Having been friends and previously business partners for over a decade, Dorothy Day and Brighdie Grounds officially launched their swimwear brand Palm in 2016.

When they first met, the women bonded over a mutual love for surfing and fashion, and they eventually started a lifestyle magazine together. A passion for merging their interests later evolved into creating Palm. Currently Palm has opened two retails stores in both Malibu and Maui.

Our two newest additions to the Palm family include Kinga Gyorkei (left) and Whitney Grounds (right).
