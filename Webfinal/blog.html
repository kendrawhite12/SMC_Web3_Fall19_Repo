ALMOND X SURFRIDER FOUNDATION
Written By Dave Allee - August 20 2019

The responsibility to protect the ocean is all of ours. That is why we’ve teamed up the stoked craftsmen over at Almond Surfboards to produce a collection of limited edition apparel and one-of-a-kind boards. The two limited edition R series surfboards are 100% recyclable foam boards and are med in the USA. A portion of sales from every board and piece of apparel will directly fund our work to keep our water clean and our beaches healthy.

We are partnering with Surf Splendor Podcast to give away two Almond x Surfrider R-Series boards. A lucky winner will get the 5'4 Secret Menu, and ANOTHER lucky winner will get the 8'0 Joy. We are proud to collaborate with our friends at the Surfrider Foundation to create a curated collection of surfboards, apparel and accessories with a portion of the proceeds supporting Surfrider's mission to protect our ocean, waves and beaches. Enter for a chance to win one of either of the R-Series Surfboards we did with Surfrider. Winner will be drawn and announced December 30th 2019.

LAUNCHING THE 2020 R SERIES COLLECTION

Last year, we launched the R-Series, an entirely new construction of your favorite Almond Surfboard models. The enthusiasm for the USA-made, 100% recyclable foamie far exceeded our wildest dreams.

The most common question we received over the last year was "this is great, but when is a longer one coming!?". Well friends, the wait for the longer R-Series surfboard is almost over... we are very proud to introduce the next big thing in the foam surfboards...

GQ BEST STUFF: R SERIES SURFBOARD
Written By Dave Allee - December 02 2018

We are very honored that GQ Magazine selected the R-Series Surfboard as a part of their Best Stuff of 2018.The CHART print 5'4 Secret Menu is featured in the Best Stuff section inside the December issue of the magazine. The board will also be on display in Los Angeles for their Best Stuff pop-up store this month. Huge thank you to Sam and the GQ editors for their interest in—and support of—our effort to make the best foam surfboards possible.
